# Intent Engineering Audit

1. Read the `intent-engineering` skill documentation to ensure you have the latest principles in context: use `view_file` on `/Users/mac/Downloads/2026/skills/skills/intent-engineering/SKILL.md`.
2. Review the current task, project, or agent design against the **Plan-Implement-Validate (PIV) Loop** structure.
3. Verify that you are not just executing instructions, but understanding the **Machine-Readable Intent**. Check if the objectives are **Agent-Actionable** (signals, data sources, authorized actions) rather than just human-readable OKRs.
4. Ensure you have defined the 7 essential components: Objective, Desired Outcomes, Health Metrics, Strategic Context, Constraints, Decision Autonomy, and Stop Rules.
5. Identify the targeted level of **AI Autonomy** (Operator, Collaborator, Consultant, Approver, Observer) and ensure your alignment requirements match the level.
6. Provide a markdown checklist to the user summarizing your intent engineering alignment, explicitly listing the 7 components and any gaps or violations of the principles outlined in `SKILL.md`. Stop and wait for the user's feedback or confirmation before proceeding.
